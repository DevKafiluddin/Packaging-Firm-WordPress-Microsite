    About Ironclad

    ORGANIZED SOLUTIONS FOR A DISORGANIZED WORLD

    IronClad Hardware started in a small warehouse in 1994 with a simple mission: eliminate the chaos of loose hardware. Today, we are industry leaders in packaging systems, providing organized, labeled, and quality-assured kits to businesses worldwide.

    Our state-of-the-art facility utilizes automated counting and packaging systems to ensure 100% accuracy in every kit we ship. We don't just sell hardware; we sell efficiency.
